# ISC2 Malaysia Chapter Annual Membership
Thank you for your interest in becoming an ISC2 Malaysia Chapter member. We have now moved to the new Official ISC2 Chapter membership platform, sponsored by ISC2 itself. To register, kindly perform the following steps:

1. Head to the new ISC2 Malaysia Chapter portal at https://malaysia.isc2chapters.isc2.org/
2. Click Log in in the top-right corner. You’ll be redirected to the ISC2 login portal.
![Join the Chapter](/join-the-chapter-1.png)
3. Sign in with your ISC2 account or create a new account if you don’t have one (no ISC2 certification is required to create an account - non-certified members are fully supported by clicking on the “Sign Up” button).
![Join the Chapter](/join-the-chapter-2.png)
4. After signing in, you’ll be prompted to complete a short registration form.
5. Once successfully registered, you’ll return to the ISC2 Malaysia Chapter portal.
6. To join the chapter, click on the Memberships tab from the horizontal menu, then tick the “Select Membership” checkbox under One Time Registration Fee. Click the Proceed to payment button in the bottom-right corner. All members are required to make a one-time payment of RM50 as a registration fee. You can refer to the Membership details at https://www.isc2chapter.my/membership/.
7. Proceed with the payment steps until successful.
8. Once you’ve completed the registration fee payment, you can now choose which type of membership you would like to subscribe to (Professional, Associate or Student). The benefits and differences of each membership plan are explained under that plan.
9. Once you’ve decided, you can proceed with the respective membership payment. The annual fee is RM100 for Professional members, RM50 for Associate members and RM30 for Student members.
10. Congratulations! You are now an official member of the ISC2 Malaysia Chapter!
